About Us

Meet Team Garud, a highly motivated group of students from NIT Bhopal making significant strides in the world of robotics! Actively engaged in diverse projects and organized impactful workshops.
We've conducted hands-on robotics workshops for students from DAVV Indoer, SATI Vidisha(focusing on RC car making),Medicaps Indore College, and Ujjain engineering College(featuring line Follower Bot practicals),reaching over 200+ students.
Our competitive spirit has le to impressive results, including winning AutoMax'25(MANIT), being finalists at Shaashtra'25(IIT Madras), winning TechneX'25(IIT BHU), placing third in AeroGlide(MANIT), and Placing Second in RoboRun'25(MANIT)
